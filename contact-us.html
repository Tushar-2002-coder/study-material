📖 How to Use This Portal

📋
SyllabusGo to Materials → Syllabus. Select your semester and click Submit to download the official SCTEVT syllabus PDF.
📚
BooksVisit Materials → Books. Choose from the dropdown list of 21 reference books and click Submit to open the PDF.
📄
Question PapersGo to Materials → Question Papers. Filter by semester, subject, and year. Click "Show All" to browse everything.
📖
Notes PDFUnder Materials → Notes PDF, UGIE Notes, or DTET Notes. Select semester then subject to open the corresponding notes.
📤
Contribute Question PapersOn the Dashboard, click "Submit via Google Form" to share your exam papers and help your juniors.
