About This Website

     Welcome to Study Material Tushar, a dedicated educational platform designed exclusively for Diploma in Chemical Engineering students.


This website is created to support diploma students by providing organized, semester-wise academic resources that make exam preparation easier and more efficient.


Whether you're in your first year or final semester, you’ll find helpful content tailored to your syllabus.


Our goal is to offer easy access to subject-wise notes, previous year question papers, important questions, and study materials for all semesters of the Chemical Engineering diploma course.


Each section is neatly categorized so you can quickly find the materials you need without wasting time.


Whether you're reviewing for class tests, semester exams, or board exams, this platform is your complete academic companion.


In addition to study resources, the website includes a "Help" section where students can ask questions or seek guidance related to chemical engineering topics, career options, and practical training.


We're building a strong academic community where students can grow together and help each other succeed.


Study Material Tushar is created by a student, for students — with real experience, real needs, and real solutions in mind.


It’s not just a study site, but a growing platform to support the journey of every diploma-level chemical engineering student striving for success in academics and beyond.
