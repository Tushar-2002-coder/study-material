About the Portal

Built by a Student,
for Students

A dedicated educational platform for Diploma in Chemical Engineering students — built with real experience and real needs in mind.

🎯

Our Mission

To provide every Chemical Engineering diploma student with free, organised, and exam-ready academic resources — regardless of their location or financial background.

We believe that quality study material should be accessible to all — not locked behind expensive books or coaching centres.

📚

What We Offer

Comprehensive resources covering all 6 semesters of the Diploma in Chemical Engineering curriculum — notes, previous year question papers, books, lab manuals, and syllabus documents.

All materials are categorised semester-wise, subject-wise, and topic-wise for quick navigation.

🤝

Community-Driven

This platform grows with contributions from students like you. When you share your exam papers, you help hundreds of juniors who come after you.

We're building a strong academic community where students collaborate, contribute, and succeed together.

🏛️

Institutional Coverage

Resources are curated for institutes under SCTEVT Odisha, covering both UGIE and DTET patterns.

Whether you're preparing for class tests, semester exams, or board examinations — this portal is your complete academic companion.
